## Add category images here

### Naming convention:
- Use lowercase letters
- No spaces (use underscore instead)
- No special characters
- Format: `{category_name}.jpg`

### Examples:
- electronics.jpg
- phones.jpg
- computers.jpg
- accessories.jpg

### Image specifications:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 400x400px recommended
- Square ratio (1:1)
